ⓘ What do the Roman numerals mean?

Roman numerals number the chords in a key. Uppercase = Major (bright), lowercase = minor (darker).

#NumeralTypeIn key of GFeel
1IMajorGHome base
2iiminorAmStepping stone
3iiiminorBmMoody
4IVMajorCThe lift
5VMajorDTension / wants to resolve
6viminorEmThe emotional one

The chord names change with the key, but the pattern stays the same. I-V-vi-IV always feels like "home, tension, sad, lift" no matter what key you play it in.
